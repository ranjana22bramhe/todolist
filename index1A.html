Instructions

* Click on the "Add Task" button or input field to create a new to-do item.

* Type in your task description and press Enter or click "Add."

* To mark a task as complete, click the checkbox next to the task.

* Completed tasks will be displayed with a strikethrough effect.

* Click on the task text to edit its content.

* Make changes directly in the text field and press Enter to save.

* Click the "Remove" or "Delete" button next to a task to remove it from the list.

* Use the "Important" checkbox to mark tasks as high priority.

* If you accidentally remove or complete a task, use the "Undo" button to restore it.

* Mark tasks with a reminder checkbox to set a due date and time.

* Use the navigation icons or buttons to switch between different sections of the to-do list, such as "Today," "Upcoming," and "Completed."

Symbols

  • Add a new task to the list.
  • Undo your last action.
  • Delete a task from the list.
  • Add or view details and notes for a task.
  • Set a reminder for a task.

Benefits

* A to-do list helps organize tasks in a clear and manageable way, reducing the feeling of being overwhelmed.

* By listing tasks, you can prioritize and focus on completing them one by one, boosting productivity.

* A to-do list allows you to allocate time effectively, ensuring that important tasks are completed within deadlines.

* Writing down tasks helps to clear your mind, reducing stress and anxiety about forgetting something important.

* You can easily rearrange tasks or add new ones as your day progresses, adapting to changes and maintaining control over your workload.

* Breaking down larger goals into smaller tasks on a to-do list makes it easier to achieve long-term objectives.

* A to-do list helps you stay focused on what needs to be done, reducing distractions and increasing concentration.
